Here's the fastest route from level 500 all the way to the Third Sea.

🙏 Buddha — The Grinding King

Buddha is S+ tier for PvE grinding. Massive hitbox, hits entire mobs without aiming, 40% damage reduction. Get it by Level 1000 — it's worth every Beli. Pair with Superhuman or Electric Claw.

PvP: C-tier — big hitbox = easier to hit. Use Leopard or Dough for PvP instead.

⚡ Awakening Priority

  • Dough (Awakened) — Best awakened PvP fruit. Insane combo potential.
  • Buddha (Awakened) — Even faster grinding, extended hitbox.
  • Leopard — Already S-tier unawakened, awakened = broken.

🥊 Fighting Styles

Fighting styles are your martial arts — combine them with fruits for devastating combos. Unlock them in order.

🟦 First Sea — Basic Styles

👣 Dark Step

First Sea
  • Trainer: Dark Step Teacher (Shells Town area)
  • Cost: 150,000 Beli
  • Good for: PvP stuns, combo starter

💧 Water Kung Fu

First Sea
  • Trainer: Water Kung Fu Teacher (Underwater City)
  • Cost: 750,000 Beli
  • Good for: Damage, useful against Flame/Magma users

⚡ Electric

First Sea
  • Trainer: Shanks (Hot and Cold island)
  • Cost: 500,000 Beli
  • Good for: Fast attacks, paralysis

🐉 Dragon Breath

First Sea
  • Trainer: Dragon Breath Teacher (Cafe)
  • Cost: 500,000 Beli
  • Good for: AoE fire damage

🟩 Second Sea — Advanced Styles

💪 Superhuman

Second Sea
  • Trainer: Martial Arts Master (Café, Second Sea)
  • Cost: 3,000,000 Beli
  • Requirements: 300 mastery in Dark Step + Water Kung Fu + Dragon Breath + Electric
  • Good for: Best all-around style before Third Sea — great damage, stuns, combos

🔱 Sharkman Karate

Second Sea
  • Trainer: Underwater, Second Sea
  • Cost: 2,500,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • Requirements: Level 1,000+
  • Good for: Water-based combos, sea beast hunting

🔴 Third Sea — Elite Styles

⚡ Electric Claw

Third Sea
  • Trainer: Rip_Indra (Port Town, Third Sea)
  • Cost: 3,000,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • Requirements: 400 mastery in Electric
  • Good for: Top-tier PvP, fastest M1s, sea beast damage

🐲 Dragon Talon

Third Sea
  • Trainer: Uzoth (Haunted Castle, Third Sea)
  • Cost: 3,000,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • Requirements: 400 mastery in Dragon Breath
  • Good for: High damage combos, works with Dragon fruit

🩸 Sanguine Art

Third Sea
  • Trainer: Third Sea (Secret location)
  • Cost: High Beli + Fragments
  • Good for: SS-tier PvP, life-steal, insane combo extension
  • Currently one of the strongest styles in the game

👑 God Human

Third Sea
  • Trainer: Elevated (Third Sea)
  • Cost: 5,000,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • Requirements: 400 mastery in Superhuman + Electric Claw + Dragon Talon + Sharkman Karate + Death Step
  • Total cost to unlock all prerequisites: ~20.4M Beli + 26,500 Fragments
  • Good for: THE best fighting style — best damage, best combos, best PvP
🎯 Unlock Order: Dark Step → Electric → Dragon Breath → Water Kung Fu → Superhuman (Second Sea) → Electric Claw → Dragon Talon → God Human (endgame)

⚔️ Secret Weapons

🎸 Soul Guitar (Skull Guitar)

Level 2,300+ 5,000,000 Beli 250 Ectoplasm 500 Bones 1 Dark Fragment

📍 Location: Haunted Castle Island — Third Sea. Only available on a full moon (every 8 in-game days, or every 3 days in a private server).

1
Zombie Challenge — Kill 6 Living Zombies simultaneously with one AoE attack. They spawn near the red lake on the far side of the island. Lure all 6 together, then blast them at once.
2
Gravestone Puzzle — Go to the graveyard. Two rows of signs lead to the castle entrance. Count the gravestones next to each sign. Click the sign with the MOST gravestones next to it.
3
Trophy Puzzle — Go to the garden (between the castle and Soul Reaper room). Memorize/screenshot the trophy order facing the castle. Then go to the electrical room under the castle → circuits room → enter the code matching the trophy directions.
4
Pipe Puzzle — Look at the tiles on the floor. Click tiles until their colors match the colors of the pipes on the wall and ceiling.
5
Craft — Complete all steps → go to the Weird Machine in the castle basement → hand over your materials → receive the Soul Guitar!

⚔️ Cursed Dual Katana (CDK)

Level 2,200+ Yama — 350+ mastery Tushita — 350+ mastery

📍 Location: Mansion — Third Sea. Talk to the Crypt Master NPC next to the big stone building on the hill behind the Mansion.

1
Get Both Swords — Obtain Yama and Tushita and level both to 350+ mastery. This takes time — don't skip it.
2
Find Crypt Master — He's behind the Mansion on the hill. Talk to him to start the puzzle.
3
Two Scrolls, Six Trials — Inside the Mansion are 2 scrolls, each with 3 trials. Complete the Yama trials with Yama equipped, and Tushita trials with Tushita equipped.
4
Collect Alucard Fragments — Finishing all 6 trials gives you 6 Alucard Fragments. Place them on the pedestal at the front of the Mansion.
5
Defeat the Boss — A Level 2,025 Cursed Skeleton Boss spawns. You can ONLY damage it with Yama or Tushita. Beat it → 100% drop rate for the CDK!
💡 CDK Tips: The boss is hard — bring a friend. Make sure you don't accidentally switch weapons. The CDK is one of the most powerful swords in the game — worth the grind!

🧩 Puzzle Hints

Quick cheat sheet for every major puzzle in Blox Fruits.

🏴‍☠️ Darkbeard Awakening

  • Collect 30 Magma Ore (from Fire Island)
  • Collect 30 Leather (from Swan Pirates)
  • Collect 20 Scrap Metal (from Camels)
  • Deliver to Blacksmith NPC in Second Sea to forge Raid Chip

🐉 Rip_Indra Portal (Electric Claw unlock)

  • Talk to Rip_Indra at Port Town
  • Need 400 Electric mastery
  • Pay 3,000,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • He's only accessible after defeating the Sea Beast raid

👑 God Human Unlock Puzzle

  • Reach Third Sea + Level 2,300+
  • Have 400+ mastery in: Superhuman, Electric Claw, Dragon Talon, Sharkman Karate, Death Step
  • Bring 5,000,000 Beli + 5,000 Fragments
  • Talk to the Elevated teacher in Third Sea

🎸 Soul Guitar Puzzle

  • Wait for full moon at Haunted Castle
  • Kill 6 zombies at once (AoE attack)
  • Click gravestone sign with most graves
  • Match trophies → enter circuit code
  • Match pipe tile colors to wall pipes
  • Craft at the Weird Machine in the basement

⚔️ Cursed Dual Katana Puzzle

  • Need Yama + Tushita at 350+ mastery each
  • Find Crypt Master behind Mansion
  • Complete 6 trials with the correct sword equipped
  • Place 6 Alucard Fragments on the pedestal
  • Beat the Lv. 2025 boss (only Yama/Tushita can damage it!)

🌊 Sea Beast Hunting

  • Spawn by dealing damage to sea monsters while sailing
  • Use Electric Claw or Godhuman M1 to hit them underwater
  • Good drops: Fragments, Shark Tooth, Sea Beast materials
  • Best done in a group of 2-3 players

💡 Pro Tips

📋

Always Have a Quest

Quests give WAY more XP than just killing mobs. Always grab a quest from the Quest Giver NPC before grinding.

🔄

Server Hop

If mobs are being taken by other players, hop to a new server. Less competition = faster respawns = faster leveling.

2x

Use 2x XP Events

When there's a 2x XP event, grind as hard as you can. You level up twice as fast!

🍊

Get Buddha ASAP

Buddha fruit makes grinding so much easier. If you see it in the shop, buy it. It's worth every Beli.

🥋

Fighting Style Matters

Get Superhuman in Second Sea. Then work toward Electric Claw or God Human in Third Sea.

🌊

Rush Second Sea at 700

Don't hang around First Sea too long. Get to Level 700, unlock Second Sea — XP gains are much better.

👥

Party Up

Friend has Buddha? Let them tank mobs while you deal damage. Faster kills, shared quest XP.

🏪

Check the Shop Daily

The Fruit Dealer stock changes every 4 hours. Rare fruits like Leopard or Dragon can appear — always check!
