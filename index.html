🗺️ Level 800 Guide

Level 800 is the transition between Sea 1 and Sea 2 — here's everything you need to do

🌊

Where are you at level 800?

At level 800 you're at the transition between Sea 1 and Sea 2. Recommended to move to Sea 2 at level 800–1000. Farm to at least 775 before going.

🎯

Where to grind at 800?

Best zones for level 800:

  • Kingdom of Rose — levels 700–850
  • Underwater City — levels 800–925
  • Graveyard Island — levels 950–1000
🍑

Best fruit for grinding

Buddha is the king of grinding. Giant form = massive reach on all attacks. +55% damage reduction when awakened. Water walking.

⚡

What to unlock at 800

  • Activate Buso Haki (Armament)
  • Start hunting Meito swords (legendary)
  • Collect Fragments for awakening
  • Complete the Saber Expert quest
  • Find Poncho NPC for Instinct V2
🏆

Awakening

Once you collect enough Fragments, awaken your fruit at Coastal God. Awakened fruits have massively better abilities. Buddha awakened = +8x attack reach!

💎

Haki System

Buso Haki — hard armor, attacks damage Logia fruit users. Get at Ability Teacher.
Ken Haki (Instinct) — dodge attacks, see hidden enemies.

✨ Effects & Systems

Key systems you need to understand to progress in the game

🖤

Buso Haki (Armament)

Hard armor — without it you can't damage Logia fruit users. Higher Haki level = more damage and range. Mandatory for Sea 2.

📍 Get from: Ability Teacher · Cost: 25,000 Beli
👁️

Ken Haki / Instinct (Observation)

Auto-dodge attacks. Every attack has a % chance of auto-dodge. Instinct V2 can see hidden and invisible enemies. Unlocks gradually.

📍 Upgrade through combat — more experience = better dodge %
🛡️

Logia Immunity

Elemental fruits (Flame, Ice, Light, Sand…) grant immunity to physical attacks. Enemy must have Buso Haki to damage you.

⚠️ Logia immunity fails in water!
⚡

Awakening

Collect Fragments from bosses and Raids, then awaken your fruit at Coastal God. Awakened abilities are massively stronger — Buddha +8x reach!

📍 Fragments: Boss drops, Raid rewards, Sea Chests
🌊

Water Walking

Some fruits (Buddha, Phoenix) allow walking on water. Fruits otherwise drain health in water. Without special fruits, water = death.

💡 Dragoon Trident and some swords also grant water walking
🔨

Blacksmith — Upgrades

At the Blacksmith NPC upgrade your sword or fruit with materials for higher damage. Up to 10 upgrades per sword. Dragon Talon Sage enchants with unique properties.

📍 Blacksmith: Shells Town · Dragon Talon: Sea 3
📈

Mastery

Every weapon and fruit has a Mastery level — higher = new ability unlocked. Grind with the weapon you want to develop. Mastery 300 unlocks the 3rd ability.

💡 Auto-clicker ≤800ms for faster Mastery
🎁

Fruit Spawns

Fruits spawn randomly on the map every hour. Mythical <1% chance. Blox Fruits Dealer sells a random fruit for Robux/Beli every hour.

🎲 Mythical at Dealer = 2,900,000 Beli or 2,550 R$
✈️

Flight

Fruits that enable flight: Light, Dragon, Phoenix, Rumble, Spin, Falcon, Spirit, Kitsune. Flight greatly simplifies island travel and grinding.

💡 Light is the fastest flight in the game
