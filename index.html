🙏 Buddha — The Grinding King

Buddha is S+ tier for PvE grinding. Its transformation gives you a massive hitbox, hits entire mobs without aiming, and gives 40% damage reduction. If you don't have Buddha by level 1000 — get it immediately. Pair with Superhuman or Electric Claw fighting style for maximum damage.

Note: Buddha is C-tier in PvP — the big hitbox makes you easier to hit. Use Leopard or Dough for PvP.

⚡ Awakening Priority

  • Dough (Awakened) — Turns a strong fruit into a broken one. Best awakened PvP fruit.
  • Buddha (Awakened) — Even faster grinding, extended hitbox range.
  • Leopard — Already strong unawakened, awakened = top tier everything.

🌊 Second Sea Guide (Level 700–1500)

How to Unlock

  1. Reach Level 700
  2. Talk to the Military Detective NPC
  3. Get the special key
  4. Go to the cave in Frozen Village
  5. Defeat the Ice Admiral
  6. Portal to Second Sea opens!

Key Locations

  • 🏰 Ice Castle — Best grinding spot, farm Awakened Ice Admiral
  • 🏙️ Kingdom of Rose — Main hub, quests and upgrades
  • 🏝️ Hydra Island — Mid-second sea area
  • ⚠️ Forgotten Island — Skip it, bad XP

Don Swan (Level 1000 Boss)

  • Give Trevor a fruit worth $1,000,000+
  • Uses Spider fruit — 2 phases
  • Phase 1: Multi-Spider attack (X shape) + Ultimate Thread
  • Phase 2: Much faster, Awakened Ultimate Thread (3-way), Overheated Sniper
  • Tip: Use ranged moves, stay mobile

🌋 Third Sea Guide (Level 1500+)

How to Unlock

  1. Reach Level 1500
  2. Complete questline with King Red Head
  3. Defeat Don Swan
  4. Take Mr. Captain's boat from Docks 3

What's in Third Sea

  • 🗺️ New islands and much harder enemies
  • ⚔️ Awakening available for more fruits
  • 🏆 Raids for gear and upgrades
  • 💪 Level cap: 2450
  • 🌟 God Human fighting style (endgame)

💡 Pro Tips

📋

Always Have a Quest

Quests give WAY more XP than just killing mobs. Always grab a quest from the Quest Giver NPC before grinding.

🔄

Server Hop

If mobs are being taken by other players, hop to a new server. Less competition = faster respawns = faster leveling.

2x

Use 2x XP

When there's a 2x XP event in the game, grind as hard as you can. You can level up twice as fast!

🍊

Get Buddha ASAP

Buddha fruit makes grinding so much easier. If you see it in the shop, buy it. It's worth every Beli.

🥋

Fighting Style Matters

Learn Superhuman or Electric Claw — they deal massive damage and have great combos with top fruits.

🌊

Rush Second Sea at 700

Don't hang around in First Sea too long. Get to Level 700, unlock Second Sea, and the XP gains get much better.

👥

Party Up

Grinding with a friend who has Buddha? Let them tank the mobs while you deal damage. Faster kills, shared quest XP.

🏪

Check the Shop Daily

The Blox Fruit dealer's stock changes every hour. Rare fruits like Leopard or Dough can appear — always check!
